4. Script Connections & Data Passing
•
Who creates things? As you correctly noted, GameInitializer bootstraps everything and injects dependencies (like the Configs and the AR Managers).
•
How do they communicate? Using the EventBus<T>. Services do not hold references to each other. They shout into the void (Publish), and anyone who cares listens (Subscribe).
•
ScriptableObjects: These hold our variables (like CeilingHeight or SpawnRate). They live in the Project folder, not the scene. This means zero memory duplication. They are our immutable (unchanging) data models.
